Our Story

Three Generations, One Promise

Founded in 1977, Saldana's Exhaust and Muffler has been a fixture on Lacey Blvd for nearly five decades. What started as a family dream has grown into one of the most trusted names in Kings County auto repair.

Today the shop is led by RosanaMarie Saldana, carrying forward the same values her family built the business on: honest pricing, quality work, and treating every customer like a neighbor.

When you bring your vehicle to Saldana's, you're not a number — you're part of a community we've served since 1977.
